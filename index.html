Who am I?



My name is Erik Blomquist, I am a recent IT graduate from CSUN and a certified AWS Cloud Practitioner. Born and raised in Sweden but currently living in Los Angeles (since 2011) with my wife and four cats . I am passionate about Amazon Web Services and have 2 years of experience in securing, deploying, monitoring and migrating applications to a modern economic friendly and scalable solution in the cloud. I am currently working to obtain the AWS Solutions Architect - Associate certificate while learning more about the latest and greatest DevOps tools.
